    Kristen McAtee is an American YouTuber and social media influencer who first came into the spotlight via guest appearances on the video clips of Viners such as Scotty Sire and Toddy Smith. After the platform was shut down in 2017, she worked on her self-titled YouTube channel which revolves around vlogs, challenge videos, and non-traditional cooking recipe videos. The channel has amassed more than 1.2 million subscribers. She is popular on other social media platforms as well with more than 550k followers on Twitter and more than 1.5 million followers on Instagram. In 2019, Kristen joined the cast of a web show named The Reality House. She also has more than 400k followers on TikTok.
