    LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA: 'The Golden Wedding' stars Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist are all set to take the big plunge.

    Gerry and Theresa are ready to say "I Do" in front of their friends and family members at a California wedding.

    Not long ago, the pair shared their wedding registry on Amazon, according to a report by The US Sun.

    On their social media page, both of them posted, "Thrilled to unveil our Amazon Wedding Registry. Our carefully curated list aims to inspire you and your loved ones on your unique and special occasions!"

    Their wedding registry consists of a wide range of products including kitchen utensils, coffee makers, picture frames, and technology.

    The items mentioned by the couple in their registry are as follows: A Smeg Retro Style Coffee Maker Machine in the color cream - $229, A Samsung 55-inch Art Mode Smart TV - $1,197, A Deco TV Gold Frame for their Samsung 55-inch Smart TV - $599, A Slothmore Back Massager - $140, A Sonos Move Battery Powered Smart Speaker - $369, and Recyo Cable Knit White Throw Blanket - $33.

    Who is officiating Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist's Golden Wedding?

    Susan Noles is officiating Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist's Golden Wedding.

    For the unversed, Noles who competed for Turner's heart on 'The Golden Bachelor' recently announced that she will be serving as the officiant at the pair's wedding on her Instagram page. 

    Noles shared a delightful photo of herself with the iconic couple on her Instagram. Taking to her Instagram page, she expressed, "The secret is out! I'm officiating THE GOLDEN WEDDING, and I'm absolutely thrilled!"

    When did 'The Golden Bachelor' stars Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist get engaged?

    Gerry and Theresa got engaged during the grand finale of the ABC show 'The Golden Bachelor' that saw the light of day on November 30, 2023.

    Turner "presumably proposed" to the love of his life during the week of August 30, 2023, as per a report by Cosmopolitan. 

    It has been revealed by the media outlet US Weekly, that the couple got engaged in Costa Rica. 

    Did Theresa Nist keep her engagement a secret from her sister?

    Theresa and Gerry who are ready to get married on live television had to keep their engagement a secret for a long time.

    From the moment they captured their engagement in August until the show's finale aired in November 2023, Nist refrained from disclosing the events to her sister, even when a blogger named Reality Steve leaked the ending.

    While having a chat with The Wrap, Nist said, "I have a sister who I wouldn’t even tell that we were together. She was in the hometown and she met him and it drove her out of her mind."

    She went on to say, "Even when Reality Steve leaked it, I said, ‘You know, he’s not always right, you have to watch the show!’”
