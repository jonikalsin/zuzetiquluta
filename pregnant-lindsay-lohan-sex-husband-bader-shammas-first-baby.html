    • TMZ reported that the actress is set to give birth to her first child very soon.

    Lindsay Lohan is having a baby boy

    Lindsay Lohan is going to be a boy mom really soon.

    The Mean Girls star and her husband Bader Shammas are expecting a baby boy, as per TMZ‘s report on Monday.

    The publication also reported that the 36-year-old actress’s mother will fly to Dubai to be by her daughter‘s side for the birth.

    The outlet added,

    “And some of Lindsay’s siblings will also be overseas around the same time as the birth to meet their new nephew.”

    The Parent Trap actress who has been residing in Dubai for a couple of years announced her pregnancy with her friends and family back in March.

    Her rep said at the time,

    “She is feeling great and she is thrilled,”

    The following month, the actress’s family threw a lavish baby shower in New York City which was also attended by her brother Dakota and sister Aliana.

    Lohan also revealed the very unexciting way in which she revealed her pregnancy to her husband.

    Earlier this month, she told Allure magazine,

    “I walked into the room, I threw the test down. I was like, ‘Guess what?’ He goes, ‘We are?’”

    “I can’t wait to see what the feeling is and what it’s like to just be a mom.”

    Lindsay has not revealed her due date yet but she did tell the magazine that her pregnancy has caused her to be very emotional in recent weeks.

    She said,

    “Happy tears. That’s just who I am,”

    “Though now, it’s probably baby emotion.”

    Lindsay and Bader’s short relationship timeline

    In July 2022, news of Lindsay and Shammas’s secret marriage became public. The couple met after she moved to Dubai in 2018.

    A source confirmed at the time,

    “I can confirm Lindsay Lohan is married.”

    Lohan seemingly confirmed the news by sharing a photo of her and the businessman writing,

     “I am the luckiest woman in the world. He found me and knew that I wanted to find happiness and grace, all at the same time. I am stunned that this is my husband. My life and my everything. Every woman should feel like this everyday.”

    The Kuwait-born financier popped the question in November with a massive Harry Winston diamond sparkler. However, they announced the news four months later in March 2022.

    Lohan’s dad, Michael Lohan was also pretty impressed by his new son-in-law and said at the time,

     “He’s not a Hollywood type, you don’t see any pictures of them in the press. That was one of Lindsay’s problems — the paps encroaching on her and coming up with stories. It was tough. But she’s with a guy who doesn’t like the limelight.”

    He added,

    “She’s relaunching her career and you want a good person in your life when you hit that restart button,”

    “Everyone’s happy. Lindsay got her life back when she met him, she’s been living a really happy and healthy life.”
