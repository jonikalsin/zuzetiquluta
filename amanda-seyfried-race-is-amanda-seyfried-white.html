    Amanda Seyfried is an actress, singer, and model from the United States.

    Amanda began modeling at the age of eleven and began acting at the age of fifteen, with recurring parts as Lucy Montgomery on CBS’s As the World Turns (1999–2001) and Joni Stafford on ABC’s All My Children (2003).

    Jack Seyfried is her father’s name, and Ann Seyfried is her mother’s name. Jennifer Seyfried, her sister, is her other sibling.

    Her feature film debut in the teen comedy Mean Girls (2004) and her recurring role as Lilly Kane on the UPN television series Veronica Mars (2004–2006) catapulted her to fame.

    Amanda went on to play minor roles in independent films including Nine Lives (2005).
