    Introduced in Generation II

    The Twisted Spoon (Japanese: まがったスプーン Twisted Spoon), formatted TwistedSpoon prior to Generation VI, is a type of held item introduced in Generation II that boosts the power of Psychic-type moves.

    In the core series games

    Price

    Effect

    Generations II and III

    Boosts the power of the holder's Psychic-type moves by 10%.

    Generation IV onward

    Boosts the power of the holder's Psychic-type moves by 20%.
