    Billy Busch Sr. Net Worth: $200 Million

    Billy Busch Sr. is an American businessman and reality television personality who gained a net worth of $200 million. He was born in St. Louis, Missouri and is a member of the Busch family known for beer. His great grandfather was Adolphus Busch. Although he never worked for the family business, he founded his own beer company Kraftig which eventually closed in 2019.

    Billy Busch Sr. gained fame for starring on the MTV reality show Busch Family Brewed. However, he has also faced legal troubles in the past. In 2018, he pleaded guilty to a peace disturbance charge after he was accused of grabbing an 11 year old boy and pushing him into a wall. In 1988, he had a custody battle with Angela Whitson who was the mother of his daughter.
