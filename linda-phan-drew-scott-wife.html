    Linda Phan, Drew Scott’s Wife

    Linda Phan is the creative director of Scott Brothers Entertainment and co-founder of Sofa Company. She is also the wife of Canadian television personality and twin brother of Property Brothers, Drew Scott. The couple tied the knot in a private ceremony in Italy on May 12, 2018.

    Linda Phan Birthday

    Linda Phan was born on September 10, 1983, in Toronto, Canada. She is of Vietnamese descent. Her parents immigrated to Canada during the Vietnam War. Linda has two brothers named Calvin and Julius. She grew up in Markham, Ontario, and graduated from Unionville High School in 2002.

    Phan attended George Brown College for Fashion Business Management and Ryerson University for Fashion Design. After graduation, she worked as a buyers’ assistant at Holt Renfrew and later as a stylist at Aritzia. She also did freelance styling for magazines such as Elle Canada and FLARE.
