    Vogt blasted a home run in his previous outing against the Angels. But it meant much more to Vogt since his three children had introduced him as he approached the plate. Vogt was appointed the Seattle Mariners’ bullpen and quality control coach earlier this year. The catcher takes over for Terry Francona, the most successful manager in Guardians history.

    Stephen Vogt Family: Wife And Three Children

    Stephen Vogt lives in Olympia, Washington, with his family. The former Seattle Mariners coach and his wife, Alyssa Vogt, have three children. The better half of the Guardians’ management has worked in a similar professional capacity as her spouse. Alyssa resigned from her post in 2017 due to family obligations. It was the year after the birth of her and Stephen’s third child, and the obligations were building up.
