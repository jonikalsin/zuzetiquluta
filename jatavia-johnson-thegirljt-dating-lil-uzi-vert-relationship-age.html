    • Jatavia Johnson known as the thegirlJT is a member of City Girls
    • She is dating rapper Lil Uzi Vert for a year and their relationship is going strong

    Jatavia Johnson aka thegirlJT and Lil Uzi Vert are still together. The couple celebrated her 29th birthday in style. He rented out Nickelodeon Studios Park at the American Dream Mall in New Jersey. For her birthday, he spent around $50,000 to rent the entire venue.

    They invited friends for the celebration and posted pictures of the friend group riding dozens of rides including a roller coaster and a Drop Zone type of attraction.

    Vert gifted her a McLaren. Lil has always been spoiling JT with gifts and love. On their first date, he gave her a bag full of cash and asked her to take as much money as she could.

    JT revealed,

    “I had a little Chanel bag and he told me to take as much as you can. I could only fit $30,000, and I was so mad. Also, I don’t want to keep talking because I don’t want it to seem like I’m bragging. I have a good man. I didn’t even know he was a good man. Similarly, I was fucking with him for almost two years before I found out he was a good man.” 

    He also gifted her a diamond ring and chain.

    Who is Jatavia Johnson aka JT?

    Jatavia Shakara Johnson is a hip-hop star known for her band City Girls. She and her singer partner Yung Miami founded City Girls and they are from Miami, Florida. They signed to Quality Control Music and released two albums Girl Code and City on Lock. 

    She was born on 3rd December 1992 in Liberty City, Miami, Florida. She is 29 years old at present.
